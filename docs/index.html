Wishart moments calculator

This is a SAGE based moment calculator for Wishart distributed random variables.

Given \( k\in \mathbb{N} \) and \( W \sim \mathrm{Wishart}(\frac{n}{2} , 2 \Sigma) \) we calculate the expectation of \[ L_{(i)} (W) = r_{(i)} (W) \sum_{j=1}^k j i_j \frac{W^j}{\hbox{tr}\,(W^j)} \] for every portrait \( (i)\in I_k = \{ (i_1,\ldots,i_k)\in\mathbb{N}^k \,:\, \sum_{j=1}^k i_j = k \} \), with \(r_{(i)}(W)\) defined as in [Letac and Massam, 2004]

Capabilities:

  • The \( \rm\LaTeX \) commands of every expression can be obtained by right-clicking in the formula and then selecting Show math as > TeX commands
  • Computations can also be made for a concrete matrix.
