Wishart moments calculator

This is a SAGE based moment calculator for Wishart distributed random variables.

Given \( k\in \mathbb{N} \) and \( W \sim \mathrm{Wishart}(n , \Sigma) \) we calculate the expectation of \[ \frac{1}{k!}L_{(i)} (W) = r_{(i)} (W) \sum_{j=1}^k j i_j \frac{W^j}{\hbox{tr}\,(W^j)} \] for every portrait \( (i)\in I_k = \{ (i_1,\ldots,i_k)\in\mathbb{N}^k \,:\, \sum_{j=1}^k i_j = k \} \), with \(r_{(i)}(W)\) defined as in [Letac and Massam, 2004].

Here \(W\) is of size \(r\!\times\! r\) where \(r\) denotes the number of portraits in \(I_k\).

Portraits indexing this random variables are considered to be sorted by dominance order. For instance, for \(k=4\) \[ (1,1,1,1) \prec (2,1,1) \prec (2,2) \prec (3,1) \prec (4) \]

We also compute the expectation of \( \frac{1}{k!}L_{(i)} (W^{-1}) \) when certain condition over \( n \) is met.

In particular, when \( (i) = (k) \), here the rightmost position of the slider, moments \( \mathbb{E}(W^{\pm k}) \) can be obtained. . With this notation, the \( \mathbb{E}(W^{\pm k}) \) correspond to the \(r\)-th position of the list of portraits. Also \(r= \mathrm{dim}\,W\).

Capabilities:

  • The \( \rm\LaTeX \) commands of every expression can be obtained by right-clicking in the formula and then selecting Show math as > TeX commands
  • Computations can also be made for a specific matrix.
